About Us

Explore Goa is a student project website crafted by Joshua Greg Colaco using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as part of a frontend development course.

Our mission is to provide a vibrant and modern tourism platform highlighting the beauty and culture of Goa. From iconic beaches and Portuguese forts to spice plantations and Goan cuisine, this site is tailored for travel enthusiasts, students, and tourism promoters.

We prioritize usability, responsiveness, and experience to help users plan their dream escape to Goa with ease.
