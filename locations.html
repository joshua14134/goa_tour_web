Explore Goa's Top Travel Destinations

From beaches to spice plantations, discover Goa like never before

Baga Beach

Famous for nightlife and water sports. Located near Calangute.

Calangute Beach

The Queen of Beaches. Great for families and seafood lovers.

Fort Aguada

Historic fort with a lighthouse and sweeping sea views.

Dudhsagar Falls

Spectacular four-tiered waterfall in Mollem National Park.

Palolem Beach

Tranquil, scenic beach perfect for yoga and relaxation.

Chapora Fort

Popular Bollywood spot with stunning sunset views.

Divar Island

Quiet escape with Portuguese charm and lush greenery.

Spice Plantations

Aromatic tours of cardamom, pepper, and vanilla farms.

Anjuna Market

Boho vibe with live music, shopping, and local art.

Miramar Beach

Peaceful city beach, great for morning walks and views.

Old Goa Churches

UNESCO site with baroque architecture and history.

Mandovi River Cruise

Enjoy evening cruises with Goan music and dance.
